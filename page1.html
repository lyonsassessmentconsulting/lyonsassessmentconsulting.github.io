Equitable Assessment
Call To Action

Written in partnership with Cognia, this report provides an introduction to the history and legacy of racism in educational assessment and offers five areas of opportunity for advancing practice: classroom assessment, large-scale test design, psychometrics, reporting, and test use. Each opportunity explores a number of strategies for interrupting patterns that perpetuate inequities. 

Suggested Citation:
Lyons, S., Johnson, M., & Hinds B. F. (2021). Equitable Assessment Call to Action. Cognia.
